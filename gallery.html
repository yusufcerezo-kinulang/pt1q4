Origin of the Legend

The Legend of the Pineapple is a Filipino folktale that explains why pineapples have many “eyes.” It teaches children to be hardworking, obedient, and respectful to their parents.

Personal Reflection

This story taught me the importance of being responsible and helping my parents at home. I realized that being lazy can lead to negative consequences. The character of Pina reminds me that I should not take my family’s efforts for granted. Through this legend, I learned to appreciate the value of hard work and obedience. It inspired me to become more helpful and respectful in my daily life.
